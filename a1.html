Curriculum

Academic Programs

Follows the Uganda National Curriculum Development Centre (NCDC) syllabus, preparing learners for PLE.

Lower Primary (P1–P4)

Foundation building in literacy, numeracy, and life skills through thematic and interactive methods.

  • Literacy (English & Local Language)
  • Mathematics
  • Social Studies & R.E.
  • Creative Arts & PE

Upper Primary (P5–P7)

In-depth subject mastery and exam preparation, with emphasis on critical thinking and practical skills.

  • English & Literature
  • Mathematics & Problem Solving
  • Integrated Science
  • Social Studies & RE

Co-Curricular & PLE Prep

Sports, clubs, music, dance, and drama alongside structured PLE revision and mock exams.

  • PLE Revision Programs
  • Debate & Writers' Club
  • Music, Dance & Drama
  • Football, Netball, Athletics
